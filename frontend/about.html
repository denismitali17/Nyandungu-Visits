About Nyandungu Eco-Park

Nyandungu Eco-Park is a remarkable conservation effort in Kigali, blending Rwanda's natural beauty with eco-friendly tourism. Visitors can enjoy serene landscapes and a variety of activities designed to foster a deeper connection with nature.
