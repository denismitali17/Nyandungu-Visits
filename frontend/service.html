Our Services

Nyandungu Eco-Park Services

Guided Tours

Visitors can explore the park through guided tours, which highlight the diverse plant species and beautiful wetlands.

Ticket Booking

Nyandungu Eco-Park supports online booking through this website, allowing visitors to plan visits and organize tours ahead of time.

Recreational and Picnic Areas

The park includes serene picnic areas and open spaces where families and groups can relax, enjoy nature, and have gatherings.
